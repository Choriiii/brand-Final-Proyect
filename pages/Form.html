Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

You can return any item within 30 days of purchase with the original receipt.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping typically takes 3–7 business days, depending on your location.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide. Shipping fees and times vary by region.

Can I change or cancel my order?

You can change or cancel your order within 24 hours of placing it by contacting support.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, all payments are processed through encrypted and PCI-compliant systems.

How can I track my order?

Once shipped, you’ll receive a tracking number via email to follow your package.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes, you can select gift wrapping at checkout for an additional fee.

What if I received a damaged or wrong item?

Please contact us within 7 days of delivery and we’ll send a replacement.

Do you have physical stores?

Currently, we operate exclusively online. Subscribe to our newsletter for updates.
